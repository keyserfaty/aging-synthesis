# Aging Synthesis

A pre-committed, evidence-weighted synthesis of 38 aging interventions.

**Snapshot date:** 2026-04-25 — locked methodology and verdicts.
**Author:** Karen Serfaty ([ORCID 0009-0007-9185-4181](https://orcid.org/0009-0007-9185-4181))
**License:** CC-BY 4.0

## Read the methodology

- **[methodology.md](methodology.md)** — locked 2026-04-24, no edits since. Pre-committed evidence tiers, verdict bands, replication standards, conflict-of-interest discounts, verdict change protocol.
- **[CALIBRATION_ANCHORS.md](CALIBRATION_ANCHORS.md)** — five anchor interventions (exercise, CR, rapamycin, NMN, resveratrol) locked into specific verdict bands before any other intervention was assessed.

The methodology and anchor files together are what makes this synthesis falsifiable: every verdict can be audited against the locked rules. Disagreements should be raised against the methodology or against the citations on each intervention page, not against the rankings (which are downstream of both).

## Audit trail

Verdict changes, unresolved questions, and disputed assessments are tracked via the repository's commit history and the [GitHub issue tracker](https://github.com/keyserfaty/aging-synthesis/issues). The locked methodology and anchor files have a single creation date and no edits since 2026-04-24.

## Contribute

Disagreements with specific verdicts are welcome and explicitly invited. The methodology has a verdict-change protocol that requires new evidence at the right tier (see methodology §7). Open an issue with: (a) the intervention, (b) the new evidence, (c) the methodology section that supports the change. The synthesis is intended to be falsifiable.
